Cobra Meal & Calorie Planner

This was my first group project in boot-camp. I worked with three other people to create a meal planner that uses API's to search recipes and store them in a weekly planner! This is a great app for busy parents, fitness enthusiasts, or just people who would like to become more organized. You are then able to enter your email and have your weekly planned meals sent to the email of your choice! Check it out!
